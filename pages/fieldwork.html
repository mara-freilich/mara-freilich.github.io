Calypso: Coherent Lagrangian Pathways from the Surface Ocean to Interior

Biological sampling lead

As a collaborator with the Calypso project, I lead sampling of the biological community structure through optical measurements, flow cytometry, 16S sequencing, and respiration rate measurements. The goal of this sampling is to describe and understand the three dimensional pathways that transport biological and biogeochemical tracers including phytoplankton, nutrients, oxygen, and carbon from the surface ocean to the thermocline and from the thermocline to the deep ocean.
